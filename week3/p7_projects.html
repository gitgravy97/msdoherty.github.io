DPSS Data Analysis

What: I collected ~2-3 years worth of public-disclosure data published by campus law enforcement.

Why: I've always been fascinated with law and crime. I've also been living on campus for most of my university experience.

How: Python used to collect data from API, mapped and reduced crime types due to variable naming conventions, Flask+Jinja2 used for making the application
